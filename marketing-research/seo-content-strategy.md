## Executive Summary

This SEO and content strategy is designed to drive organic, sustainable traffic to our AI website builder platform. We focus on India-first keyword targeting, long-tail opportunities with low competition, and content that addresses the specific pain points of freelancers, small businesses, and agencies. **Primary SEO Goal**: Achieve 10,000+ monthly organic visits within 6 months and rank in the top 10 for at least 15 target keywords.

## 7. Content Quality Guidelines

### E-E-A-T Compliance (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)

Every piece of content should demonstrate:
- **Experience**: Real examples, real screenshots, not generic stock imagery
- **Expertise**: Written by people who understand web design, not content mills
- **Authoritativeness**: Cite sources, link to data, include statistics
- **Trustworthiness**: Honest comparisons, admit competitors' strengths, realistic claims

### SEO Writing Rules

1. Target keyword in H1, first 100 words, and 1-2 H2s
2. Natural keyword density (1.5-2.5%), no keyword stuffing
3. Include 2-3 related/LSI keywords naturally
4. Minimum 1,500 words for blog posts, 3,000+ for pillar pages
5. Add FAQ sections (great for featured snippets)
6. Include comparison tables (high engagement)
7. Every post must have a clear CTA (call to action)
8. Internal link to 3+ relevant pages/posts
9. External link to 2+ authoritative sources
10. Optimized images with descriptive alt tags
