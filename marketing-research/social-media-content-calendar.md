## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### X/Twitter (@Handle)
- **Post frequency**: 2-3 per day
- **Format mix**: 40% threads, 30% single posts, 20% polls/images, 10% replies/engagement
- **Best times**: 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 6:00 PM IST
- **Hashtags**: #AIWebsiteBuilder #WebsiteBuilder #IndianStartupTech
- **Tone**: Casual, slightly provocative, data-driven

### LinkedIn (Company Page + Founder)
- **Post frequency**: 3 per week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- **Format mix**: 50% long-form text posts, 25% carousels, 25% video
- **Best times**: 10:00-11:00 AM IST (Tue-Thu)
- **Tone**: Professional, data-driven, founder-led storytelling

### Instagram (@Handle)
- **Post frequency**: 5 Reels per week + daily Stories
- **Format mix**: 70% Reels (screen recordings, demos), 20% carousels (tips), 10% Stories (polls, Q&A)
- **Best times**: 6:00-7:00 PM IST
- **Hashtags**: #AIWebsite #WebsiteDesign #SmallBusinessIndia #FreelancerIndia #NoCode

### YouTube (Channel)
- **Upload frequency**: 2 per week (Tue + Sat)
- **Format mix**: 40% tutorials (8-10 min), 30% comparisons (10-15 min), 20% showcases (3-5 min), 10% vlogs (5-8 min)
- **SEO**: Title format "How to [result] with AI Website Builder | [Year]"

## Content Repurposing Matrix

One piece of content becomes many:

```
YouTube Video (10 min tutorial)
  → X/Twitter: 5-tweet thread summarizing key points
  → LinkedIn: Text post with 3 key takeaways
  → Instagram: 60-second Reel showing the best 15 seconds
  → Blog: Written transcript version (SEO-optimized)
  → Email Newsletter: "This week's video" section
  → WhatsApp: Quick tip with link to full video
```

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Tasks (30 minutes)
1. Reply to all comments/mentions on all platforms
2. Engage with 10 accounts in the target audience (comment thoughtfully)
3. Share/retweet positive mentions
4. Monitor trending hashtags related to web design, AI, Indian business

### Weekly Tasks (1 hour)
1. Review analytics — which content performed best
2. Adjust next week's content based on data
3. Engage with new followers in DMs
4. Respond to 5 Reddit threads in r/India, r/webdesign, r/freelance
