# Launch Marketing Plan — AI Website Builder Platform

> **Project**: Website Development SaaS Platform
> **Product**: AI-powered no-code website builder + hosting at ₹199/month
> **Target Market**: India (freelancers, agencies, small businesses)
> **Date**: April 6, 2026
> **Author**: Marketing Researcher

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete go-to-market plan for launching the AI website builder platform. The strategy prioritizes organic and content-driven growth over paid advertising, leveraging our unbeatable pricing (₹199/month), AI-first differentiation, and a unique freelancer/agency reseller model that no competitor offers at this price point.

**Core Insight**: The market is saturated at the premium end ($15-$100+/month) but has a massive gap in the ultra-low-cost tier for Indian SMBs. Our model of "development cost + ₹199/month hosting" creates a powerful two-sided acquisition engine where freelancers build on our platform, and their clients become ongoing subscribers.

## 1. Target Customer Segments

### Primary: Freelancers

**Profile**: Independent web developers, designers, and digital consultants aged 22-40, operating in Tier 1 and Tier 2 Indian cities.

**Pain Points**:
- Current tools (Wix, Framer, Squarespace) are too expensive to resell profitably
- Building from scratch takes too long
- Client wants "cheap" — freelancer can't justify premium tool costs
- No good white-label reseller infrastructure at affordable pricing

**Value Proposition**: Build client sites in minutes with AI, charge ₹5,000-₹50,000 for development, client pays only ₹199/month hosting. Unlimited profit margin.

**Acquisition Strategy**: Freelancer communities (Instagram, LinkedIn, Reddit r/India), developer forums, YouTube tutorials, "build a client site in 10 minutes" demo videos.

### Secondary: Small Businesses

**Profile**: Restaurant owners, coaching centers, consultants, local shops, and service providers in India with no web presence or outdated websites.

**Pain Points**:
- Web agencies charge ₹15,000-₹50,000+ for a basic site
- Don't understand technology
- Worry about ongoing maintenance costs
- Want something that "just works"

**Value Proposition**: Professional website built with AI, hosted for less than a cup of coffee per month. No technical knowledge needed.

**Acquisition Strategy**: Local SEO, Google Business Profile, regional content marketing, word-of-mouth via freelancers who build their sites.

### Tertiary: Agencies

**Profile**: Small digital agencies (2-10 people) in India building websites for multiple clients monthly.

**Pain Points**:
- Monthly per-site licensing costs from Wix/Framer eat margins
- Time spent building standard pages manually
- Need white-label capabilities
- Want to scale without hiring more developers

**Value Proposition**: AI builds sites in minutes, not hours. ₹199/month per client site. Agency keeps 100% of development revenue.

**Acquisition Strategy**: Agency-focused landing page, LinkedIn outreach, case studies showing ROI, "how we helped Agency X 10x output" content.

## 6. Referral/Affiliate Program Structure

### Freelancer Referral Program

**Program Name**: "Build & Earn"

**Structure**:
- **Referral reward**: Both referrer and referred get 1 month free hosting
- **Volume tiers**:
  - 1-10 referrals: 1 month free each
  - 11-25 referrals: ₹500 cash per referral
  - 26+ referrals: ₹1,000 cash per referral + "Top Builder" badge

**Referral mechanism**: Unique referral link + referral code. Trackable dashboard.

### Affiliate Program (Phase 2)

**Launch**: After 3 months of operation

**Commission**: 30% of first 3 months of referred customer's hosting payment
- Customer pays ₹199/month × 3 months = ₹597
- Affiliate earns ₹179.1 per conversion
- No recurring — keeps it simple and sustainable

**Eligibility**: Open to bloggers, YouTubers, and tech influencers with 1,000+ followers/subscribers.
